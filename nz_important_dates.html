Worth Knowing

On Good Friday, Easter Sunday and Christmas Day, most shops are required by law to stay closed. Anzac Day morning (until 1pm) has the same restriction. Plan your supermarket run a day ahead if you're travelling over these dates.

Sporting Fixtures

In most of New Zealand's towns and cities (smaller than Auckland), if there is a major sporting event on, accommodation can get tricky to find. Kiwis are passionate sporting supporters, especially if the opposition is Australia, England or South Africa.

Due to the nature of the sporting calendars, if you are keen to see a game — or avoid any people congestion — I suggest you have a look at the following official websites before you book:

●Rugby — Super Rugby Pacific

Fixtures and results for the Blues, Chiefs, Hurricanes, Crusaders, Highlanders and Moana Pasifika.

superrugby.co.nz →

●Netball — ANZ Premiership

New Zealand's top domestic netball competition, featuring the Mystics, Stars, Magic, Pulse, Tactix and Steel.

anzpremiership.co.nz →

●Cricket — BLACKCAPS & Domestic

International BLACKCAPS and WHITE FERNS fixtures, plus domestic Plunket Shield and Super Smash matches.

nzc.nz/matches →

●Motorsport — Supercars Championship

The Repco Supercars Championship now runs a New Zealand double-header each April — Taupō, and Christchurch's Ruapuna Raceway.

supercars.com →

Fixture dates shift every season, so these links go straight to each sport's official live schedule rather than a date we'd have to keep updating by hand.
